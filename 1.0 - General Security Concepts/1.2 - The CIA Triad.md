####  Confidentiality  

• Certain information should only be known to certain people  
• Encryption  
• Access controls  
• Two-factor authentication  
br  


####  Integrity  

• Data is stored and transferred as intended  
• Hashing  
• Digital signatures  
• Certificates  
• Non-repudiation  
br  


####  Availability  

• Information is accessible to authorized users  
• Redundancy  
• Fault tolerance  
• Patching
